Exercises

1. Five Finger Drill

~2 min — Play up 5 fingers and back (twice per position), moving up one note each time. Focus on wrist circles: down/out toward pinky, up/in toward thumb. Both hands.

2. Chord Progressions

~4 min — Pick a song's chords. Use the "added chord strategy": learn first 2 chords, then add 3rd, then 4th. Right hand chords, left hand root notes, then together.

3. Improvisation

~4 min — Left hand alternates C and G (pinky/thumb). Right hand plays any white keys freely. Be creative and develop your own style.

4. Hand Independence

~4 min — 7 core rhythms per hand. Left thumb on C, right thumb on C, pinky on G. One hand plays steady 1-2-3-4, the other plays a rhythm pattern. Then swap hands. Progress: steady rhythms → change left hand notes (C-D-E-F-G) → combine two rhythms.
